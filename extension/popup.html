About TrustLens

What is TrustLens?

TrustLens is a browser extension that helps you identify potentially unreliable websites and misinformation. Our system relies on ratings to provide real-time trust scores for websites you visit.

How it works

  • TrustLens gets the ratings based on highly reliable and credible Media Bias / Fact Check media bias organisation and a local TrustLens database that is complied locally. This local database is updated weekly by the community.
  • TrustLens displays these ratings when you visit sites
  • Stay informed about website credibility

Privacy Settings

Privacy Controls

Logging stores only domain and timestamp locally for weekly feedback. No PII is collected.

Data Collection

TrustLens respects your privacy and only collects minimal data necessary for functionality:

  • Domain names - To provide trust ratings
  • Timestamps - For weekly feedback calculations
  • No personal information - We don't collect names, emails, or browsing history

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Enable or disable logging at any time
  • Clear your engagement history completely
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers without your consent
